Invoice Details on Product in Odoo

This apps helps user to link product with invoice and show invoice lines and invoice amount and also incoming invoice lines and incoming invoice amount on product page.

Display Invoice Quantity and Amount

User can see total count of invoice quantity and amount on product.

Link Product to Related Invoices

From smart buttons on product page user can easily access related invoice lines.

Display Incoming Invoice Quantity and Amount

User can see total count of incoming invoice quantity and amount on product.

Description

Invoice Buttons on Product

On product variant form view user can see four smart button Invoice Qty, Invoice Amount, Incoming Invoice Qty and Incoming Invoice Amount click on button to see related invoice.


Invoice Quantity

When user click on Invoice Qty button all invoice lines with current product will appear.


Invoice Amount

When user click on Invoice Amount button all invoice lines with current product will appear.


Incoming Invoice Quantity

When user click on Incoming Invoice Qty button all incoming invoice lines with current product will appear.


Incoming Invoice Amount

When user click on Incoming Invoice Amount button all invoice lines with current product will appear.
